# Diabetes and Allergy Analyzer - Image Guide

## What Type of Images to Use

The **Diabetes and Allergy Analyzer** is designed to scan medication images and analyze them for:
- **Diabetes compatibility** (Type 1, Type 2, Prediabetes)
- **Allergy risks** and potential allergic reactions
- **Food interactions** related to diabetes management

   - Example: Prescription label from pharmacy

4. **Pill/Tablet with Label**
   - Medication tablet/capsule next to its label
   - Clear text showing medication name
   - Example: Metformin 500mg tablet with label

## What the Scanner Analyzes

The scanner extracts:
- **Medication name** (brand and generic)
- **Dosage information**
- **NDC (National Drug Code)** if visible
- **Active ingredients**
